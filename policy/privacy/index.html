Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 4, 2025

Talk to Notion allows users to interact with their Notion workspace using voice or text, enabling quick actions like querying data or adding tasks naturally.

What Data We Access

To function, we temporarily access your Notion content via the official Notion API. This includes page titles, databases, tasks, and block content as required for your request.

What Data We Store

We do not store or retain any Notion data. All data is accessed on-demand, processed in memory, and never saved to our servers or third-party services.

How We Use the Data

Accessed data is only used to fulfill your request—whether it’s answering a question, updating a task, or performing a Notion operation.

Security

All interactions with Notion are secured via OAuth and encrypted HTTPS. Your credentials and content are never exposed or shared.

Third-Party Access

No third parties have access to your data. Everything stays between you and Notion, via our secure interface.
